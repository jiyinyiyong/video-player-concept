It only works on Chrome, since the HTML5 video tag was introduced.

This is my idea about a video player with minimal UI components.


There's a process bar under the player,

You may click and change it position. but I didn't make the buffer visible yet.


By hovering your mouse on the bottom of the player, you can find the control panel:

So, click "Play" or "Pause" to toggle its state.

Click the "vol" to mute and unmute, also you can scroll on it.

Scroll on the words "curr" to go forward or backword by 2 seconds.
